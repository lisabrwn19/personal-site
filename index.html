Recent Projects

Search Experience

To improve discoverability of creators we iteratively improved our search experience. We built out advanced search capabilities, started curated groups and began a creator vetting process to improve user satisfaction and decrease the need for service.

Brand Stats

When brands run influencer campaigns they measure the success of posts to inform their content creation strategy. We built out a dedicated analytics hub to surface benchmarks and insights to help drive the brands influencer strategy.

Negotiation

As we opened up our platform for self service, we learned negotiation was a big part of collaborations. We built out a negotiation feature so brands could negotiate with creators and vice versa. The result was an increase in collaborations and an increase in revenue from excess content purchases.

Feedstir

Like most people, I hate scrolling through a blog post and dodging ads to get to a recipe. So I started building Feedstir, a web application that parses recipes and allows users to save and organize them for later use.

QuickTix

I found managing my dads Blackhawks season tickets to be time-consuming and error prone. I entered a hackathon competition at the University of Illinois, won 1st place and then went on to build a team. The result was an MVP for managing season tickets.
